# Day 1 - Interview Questions

## Q1. What is Microsoft Intune?

Microsoft Intune is a cloud-based endpoint management solution used to manage, secure, and monitor devices remotely.

---

## Q2. What is an Endpoint?

An endpoint is any company-managed device such as a laptop, desktop, tablet, or mobile phone.

---

## Q3. What is MDM?

MDM (Mobile Device Management) manages the entire device.

Example:
Company-owned laptop.

---

## Q4. What is MAM?

MAM (Mobile Application Management) manages only company applications.

Example:
Outlook on a personal phone.

---

## Q5. Difference between MDM and MAM?

MDM manages the complete device.

MAM manages only company applications.

---

## Q6. Name five responsibilities of an Intune Administrator.

- Device Enrollment
- Application Deployment
- Compliance Policies
- Endpoint Security
- Windows Update Management
